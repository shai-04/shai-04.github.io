Gamified Resume

Reviewing a candidate's resume can easily become monotounous, especially if you're going through several at a time. To make it a bit less dull, I turned my resume into a mini platformer game. If you have some time on your hands, give it a shot and unwind!

Regular Resume

View the regular version of my resume. Learn more about me, where I got my education and certifications from as well as my skillset and experience as a programmer / web developer. There's also an option to download an offline copy of my resume for easy access.

Contact Me

I can't read minds, but I can provide several ways for you to directly reach out to me. Feel free to leave any compliments or complaints that you may have, as I'd love to hear what's on your mind! I typically respond within a few hours.
