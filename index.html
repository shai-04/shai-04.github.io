Gamified Resume

Reviewing a candidate's resume can easily become monotounous, especially if you're going through several at the same time. To make it a bit less boring, I turned my resume into a mini platformer game. If you have some time, give it a shot and unwind at the same time!

Regular Resume

View the regular version of my resume. learn more about me, where I got my education and certifications from as well as my skillset and experience as a programmer / web developer. There's also an option to download an offline copy of my resume for easy access.

Contact Me

I can't read minds, but I can create a contact form for you to fill out and send directly to me. Feel free to leave any compliments or complaints that you may have, as I'd love to hear what's on your mind! I typically respond within a few hours.
